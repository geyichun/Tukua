# Tukua

The simplest all-in-one image processing toolkit. Open your browser and you're ready to go. Built for operations, design, and product folks who don't want to install Photoshop just to resize a picture.

## What it does

- 🖼️ **Compress** — Batch drag-and-drop, auto-compress to target size without visible quality loss
- ✂️ **Crop & Resize** — Fixed dimensions or custom ratio, export in one click
- 🎨 **Watermark** — Text or image watermarks, works on batches
- 🔄 **Convert** — PNG ↔ JPG ↔ WebP, drag in, drag out
- 📐 **Stitch & Slice** — Horizontal/vertical stitching, grid slicing

> More tools coming. Got a feature request? Open an Issue — if it makes sense, I'll build it.

## Why Tukua

No Photoshop. No app install. No sign-up. No upload. Everything runs locally in your browser — your images never touch a server. Open the page, drop your files, click a button, download. That's it.
